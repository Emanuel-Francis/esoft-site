What to Expect?

Rough Outline

Arguably one of the most important steps in development, is figuring out why you are developing the software, design, API, etc. In our case, I like to talk to clients about why they need a website. What purpose will it serve? Who will be looking at this website? Is the information just for consumption or does it require action from the user?


Mockup

In this step we will discuss what content will go where, colors, themes, etc. It's important to have a design or theme picked before the next step because it will reduce the number of revisions that will be needed. Having link to a website that you like can also help.
